Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

How can I learn German fast with AI?
FluentGermanAI uses advanced conversational AI to simulate real-life German conversations, so you practice speaking and listening 10x faster than textbook methods. Combine that with spaced repetition and you have a system that matches how your brain actually learns.
Is FluentGermanAI free to use?
Yes — FluentGermanAI offers a generous free tier including AI conversations, spaced repetition flashcards, grammar correction, and vocabulary tracking. No credit card required.
Does the AI correct my German grammar?
Absolutely. Our Grammar Fixer provides real-time corrections with detailed explanations during conversations and in the dedicated writing tool. You'll understand exactly why each correction was made.
What German levels are supported?
We support A1 (Beginner), A2 (Elementary), and B1 (Intermediate) CEFR levels, with structured lesson paths, level-appropriate AI conversations, and official-style certification tests for each.
Can I practice German speaking with AI?
Yes! Our Voice Roleplay feature uses your microphone for real-time German conversations. Choose from scenarios like a café, job interview, or city navigation — the AI responds naturally and corrects your pronunciation in context.
How is FluentGermanAI different from Duolingo?
Duolingo gamifies repetition. FluentGermanAI uses real AI conversations, proper SM-2 spaced repetition, in-depth grammar correction, and comprehensible input stories — designed for learners who want actual fluency, not just streaks.
